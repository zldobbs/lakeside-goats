About

LIVES (Lakeside ImproVement Efficiency Software) is a software program that allows for the efficient collection and utilization of science backed data to produce reports that allow the Lakeside management team to optimize accurate decision making. With high emphasis placed on accurate decision-making, the reports created by LIVES will, in turn, increase overall profitability of Lakeside Goats on an annual basis.
